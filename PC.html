Pros and Cons
Pros:
1. Manufacturing Options
-3D Printing allows the use of printing maufactured products, including customizable products
2. Rapid Prototyping
-Products can go from being just a design to becoming an actual prototype
3. Manufacturing Speed
-Just like previous advantages, manufacturing is just as easy for a large amount of final products
4. Reduced Cost
-3D printing has become cheaper than labor in 3rd world countries. Costs are still decreasing with the potential of 3D printed homes in the near future
5. Warehousing
3D printing allows only the items that are sold to be built and shipped leaving more space in warehouses. While manufturing doesnt have to be stocked constantly to make sure that everyone has the item that they need.
6. More Jobs
Due to 3D printing, more and more engineering and technician jobs are becomeing availible in order to maintain the machines
7. Medical
-Using 3D printing for medical uses can provide a vast amount of advancement in hospitals. 3D printing can allow the manufactoring of custimizable human body parts and organs.
Cons:
1. Fewer Manufacturing Jobs
-As technology grows bigger manufactoring jobs will decrease do to the fact that robots are starting to build tools we use in our daily lives
2. Limited Materials
3D printer can only currently print materials out of plastic, resin, certain metals, and ceramic.
3. Copyright
-People would be able to create products that become conterfeit and will be imposible to determine if its fake or not
4. Dangerous Items
-3D printing allows the uses to manufacture dagerous items suck as guns, knives, any weapons that could be used to harm others
5. Useless Stuff
-3D printing can cause problems in the enviorment or with your wallet due to the printing of useless things
6. Limited Size
-Currently, 3D printers are limited with small items. In the near future maybe we will one day be able to print houses.
